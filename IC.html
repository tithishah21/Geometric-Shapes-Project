COURSE PAGE

1. The side of a square is 4 cm. Calculate its area.

Answer:
Formula: A = side^2
A = 4^2 = 16 cm^2

2. The length of a rectangle is 5 m, and its width is 3 m. Calculate its area.

Answer:
A = length * width
A = 5 * 3 = 15 m^2

3. The base of a triangle is 10 cm, and its height is 6 cm. Calculate its area.

Answer:
A = 1/2 * base * height
A = 1/2 * 10 * 6 = 30 cm^2

4. The radius of a circle is 7 m. Calculate its area.

Answer:
A = 3.1416 * radius^2
A = 3.1416 * 7^2 = 153.94 m^2

5. The radius of a sphere is 5 cm. Calculate its volume.

Answer:
V = 4/3 * 3.1416 * radius^3
V = 4/3 * 3.1416 * 5^3 = 523.6 cm^3

6. The base area of a cylinder is 28 cm², and its height is 10 cm. Calculate its volume.

Answer:
V = base area * height
V = 28 * 10 = 280 cm^3

7. The side of a cube is 3 m. Calculate its volume.

Answer:
Formula: V = side^3
V = 3^3 = 27 m^3

8. The radius of a cone is 4 cm, and its height is 9 cm. Calculate its volume.

Answer:
Formula: V = 1/3 * 3.1416 * radius^2 * height
V = 1/3 * 3.1416 * 4^2 * 9 = 150.8 cm^3

9. The diagonal of a square is 10 cm. Calculate its area.

Answer:
Formula: A = diagonal^2 / 2
A = 10^2 / 2 = 50 cm^2

10. The length of a cuboid is 8 m, width is 4 m, and height is 5 m. Calculate its volume.

Answer:
Formula: V = length * width * height
V = 8 * 4 * 5 = 160 m^3
